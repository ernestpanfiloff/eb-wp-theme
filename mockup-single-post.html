What’s the Difference Between Focus and Concentration?

March 8, 2026 - 5 min read

They’re not the same. Focus is choosing what to think about. Concentration is how long you can hold it before attention drifts.

Why this matters

When you treat focus and concentration as one skill, you solve the wrong problem. You might remove distractions but still crash after 20 minutes because your cognitive energy is low.

Simple framework

  • Protect focus before work begins.
  • Protect concentration while work is in progress.
  • Recover attention between deep work blocks.
